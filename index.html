Italian cuisine with a touch of Northern Swedish influences is a constant source of inspiration for our family business. Wood-oven baked gourmet pizzas, makes this establishment one of Stockholm's best pizzerias!
We continue to develop and update our pizzas, by always choosing the highest quality ingredients and traditional methods to have achieved a standard we can be proud of.
Our gourmet pizzas are baked according to all the rules of the art, with the basis of fresh Swedish products.
We want to offer Italian pizzas made with good ingredients from Sweden at humane prices. The ambition is for Forest Pizza to be a restaurant you can visit every day, celebrate big holidays, slip in for an after-work, or come and have a quick pizza slice of your choice for lunch.
We simply want you to feel at home!
